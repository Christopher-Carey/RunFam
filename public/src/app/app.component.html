About FitFamRun

My name is Chris, a developer and member of the FatTuesdayFAM! I love this community and wanted to lend my talents in web development to create another space for us to share our fitness journeys and enjoy some friendly competition. FitFamRun will feature a realtime leaderboard. Simply create your account, log your stats from the fitness tracker of your choice, and compete with fellow members. This website will pull the data from screenshots of your fitness tracker stats and rank your results amongst other members of the Fam — but remember, there is no real “last” place when you’re working on yourself and doing something for your health.

Through monthly competitions and special events, FitFamRun will take our fitness journeys to the next level… TOGETHER!
